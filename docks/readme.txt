Keyboard commands
Letter, E: Increase weapon volume by 1 decibel, if pressed/held down.
Letter, Q: Decrease weapon volume by 1 decibel, if pressed/held down.
Letter, W: Increase weapon volume by 5 decibels, if pressed.
Letter, S: Decrease weapon volume by 5 decibels, if pressed.
Letter, D: Increase weapon pan by 1 step, if pressed/held down.
Letter, A: Decrease weapon pan by 1 step, if pressed/held down.
Letter, C: Increase weapon pan by 5 steps, if pressed.
Letter, Z: Decrease weapon pan by 5 steps, if pressed.
Letter, I: Increase weapon pitch by 1 semitone, if pressed/held down.
Letter, K: Decrease weapon pitch by 1 semitone, if pressed/held down.
Letter, J: Increase weapon pitch by 5 semitones, if pressed.
Letter, F: Increase weapon pitch by 5 semitones, if pressed.
Letter, V: Anounce current weapon volume, if pressed.
Letter, N: Anounce current weapon pan, if pressed.
Letter, H: Anounce current weapon pitch, if pressed.
Letter, L: Resets weapon volume to it's default state, if pressed.
Letter, P: Resets weapon pan to it's default state, if pressed.
Letter, T: Resets weapon pitch to it's default state, if pressed.
Letter, G: Open the cheats menu, if pressed.
Letter, Y: Check your health stattus when aplicable, if pressed.
Letter, U: Open the characters menu, if pressed.
Letter, B: Wear/remove a drawn shield when aplicable, if pressed.
Letter, M: Check your shield stattus when aplicable, if pressed.
Spacebar: Use a drawn weapon or shield when aplicable, if pressed/held down.
Tab: Open the weapons menu, if pressed.
Enter: Open the shields menu when aplicable, if pressed.
Number row: Quickly draw weapons 1 through 15 within those slots, if pressed.
Shift plus number row: Quickly draw weapons 16 through 28 within those slots, if pressed/held down.
Right braket: Enable/disable restoring your health when aplicable, if pressed.
Backslash: Enable/disable restoring your shield strength when aplicable, if pressed.
Escape: Go back to the game's menu, if pressed.
